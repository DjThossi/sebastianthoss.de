My big 5 for life

and Purpose For Existence (PFE)

My purpose for existence

I want to help others (people / animals / nature) to have an easier life filled with love, passion and devotion.

Where all this comes from

I got this book recommended by a colleague. She told me about her "big 5 for life" and how it helps her to improve her personal way of life. I loved reading this book and can recommend it to any of my friends and family.

  • It's a nice but sad story.
  • Written in style of a novel.
  • Favourite quote: "Is it a good Museum Day?"

My big 5 for life

I was at a position in my life where I should have been perfectly happy but still something was not feeling correct. After reading the book I was thinking about my personal big 5 for life for a long time. Spending weeks of thinking what my purpose of existence is and defining my big 5 for life. It turned out that the first letter of each sentence is forming the word WORLD. If you can help me on achieving my goals feel free to contact me.
